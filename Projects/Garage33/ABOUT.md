# Garage33 — Vehicle Protection & Customization Website

## What It Is
Website for a vehicle protection and customization shop (the "green and white garage"). Services include car wrapping, paint protection film (PPF), ceramic coating, window tinting, paint correction, windshield services, and auto detailing.

## Core Functionality
- Full business website with 12+ pages
- Service catalog with detailed pages per service
- 3D car visualizer (Sketchfab integration)
- Vehicle wrap color picker (Avery Dennison colors)
- PPF visualizer
- Booking system with time slots
- Chat functionality
- FAQ system
- Partner/brand logos display
- Before/after vehicle transformations gallery
