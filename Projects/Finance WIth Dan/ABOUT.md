# Finance With Dan

## What It Is
Collaborative workspace for weekly Thursday sessions with Dan. Focused on building small, monetizable "mini-apps" or "bricks" — modular components that feed into the larger AFF (A Financial Friend) project.

## Core Functionality
- Rapid prototyping of atomic, sellable features
- Each deliverable is a standalone "brick" that can be integrated into AFF later
- Testing ground for refining the "life operating system" workflow
- Co-working capacity for mastering AI tools

## Priority & Status
- Weekly cadence (Thursdays with Dan)
- Output: small milestones, not a monolithic app
- Feeds directly into AFF as modular pieces

## Key Technical Notes
- Focus on speed: prototype fast, validate fast
- Each brick should be independently monetizable
- Serves dual purpose: revenue + R&D for AFF
