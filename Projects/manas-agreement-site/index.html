Everything we do together points toward this direction:

  • Building and refining our own Life OS setups (Claude Code, agents, skills, automation)

This isn't a side project. This is where 10x–30x growth lives. The people doing well in AI right now are already focused here. The ones who aren't will figure it out too late.

How We Work Together

You're working with Intelligence Masters as an independent contractor. You're not an employee. You control your own schedule, tools, and methods. You're free to do other work independently.

There are two sides to this:

1. Contract Work — per project

When I have project work that makes sense to hand off, I'll send it your way. Each project is its own thing — we'll agree on the scope, deliverables, and your cut before you start.

Default split on end-to-end handoffs: you get 60%, I keep 40%.

This can shift depending on the project — if I'm doing significant work on it too, or if you sourced the client yourself. We'll agree per project. You always know your number before you start.

You can say no to a project if it doesn't work for you. That's fine. Just communicate it.

2. Growing Under Intelligence Masters

Instead of building a separate business entity from scratch (which is a 24/7 job on its own, as you know), you grow your professional presence through Intelligence Masters:

  • LinkedIn — Add Intelligence Masters to your profile. You're not unemployed — you're building.
  • Content — Start posting. Even light activity. Your Claude Code Life OS can literally write posts for you. This grows you AND the company.
  • Reputation — When people ask what you're doing, you've got a real answer. The bigger Intelligence Masters grows, the more it brings in for both of us.

This is a two-way street. You contribute to the brand's visibility, the brand gives you a platform and a pipeline.
